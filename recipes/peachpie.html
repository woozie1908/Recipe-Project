Peach Pie

Classic Homemade Peach Pie

Experience the taste of summer with this classic homemade peach pie. Juicy, ripe peaches enveloped in a flaky, buttery crust make for a delightful dessert that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds fresh peaches (about 6-8 medium peaches), peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1 large egg, beaten (for egg wash)
  • 1 tablespoon coarse sugar (optional, for sprinkling)
  • 1 package (14 ounces) refrigerated pie crusts (2 crusts), at room temperature

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425째F (220째C). Place a baking sheet on the lower rack to catch any drips.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced peaches, granulated sugar, cornstarch, ground cinnamon, lemon juice, vanilla extract, and salt. Gently toss to mix well and set aside.
  3. On a lightly floured surface, roll out one pie crust to fit a 9-inch pie plate. Transfer the crust to the pie plate, allowing the excess to hang over the edges.
  4. Pour the peach filling into the prepared crust, spreading it evenly. Dot the filling with small pieces of unsalted butter.
  5. Roll out the second pie crust. You can place it over the filling as a whole or cut it into strips to create a lattice pattern. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges to seal.
  6. Brush the top crust with the beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse sugar, if desired.
  7. Place the pie on the preheated baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes. Then, reduce the oven temperature to 375째F (190째C) and continue baking for an additional 35-45 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  8. If the crust browns too quickly, cover the edges with foil to prevent over-browning.
  9. Once done, remove the pie from the oven and let it cool on a wire rack for at least 2 hours before serving. This allows the filling to set properly.
  10. Slice and enjoy your homemade peach pie!
