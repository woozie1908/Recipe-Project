Blackberry Raspberry Sorbet

Refreshing Homemade Sorbet

Indulge in this delightful and refreshing Blackberry Raspberry Sorbet, perfect for cooling off on a warm day. This recipe combines the rich flavors of blackberries and raspberries into a smooth, icy treat.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups fresh blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3/4 cup superfine sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the sour cream, superfine sugar, lemon juice, and lemon zest until well combined.
  2. In a blender, combine the blackberries, raspberries, and the sour cream mixture. Blend until smooth.
  3. Add the water to the blender and blend again until the mixture is well combined and smooth.
  4. Pour the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl to remove seeds and achieve a smoother texture.
  5. Transfer the strained mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions until it reaches a sorbet consistency.
  6. Once done, transfer the sorbet into a lidded container and freeze for an additional 2 hours to firm up.
  7. Serve scoops of the sorbet in bowls or cones and enjoy!
